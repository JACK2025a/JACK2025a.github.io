information indicating whether a belief is true or valid. | 证据；证明。 fact | Vocab. 事实 | A thing that is known or proved to be true. | 事实；真相。 opinion | Vocab. 观点 | A view or judgment formed about 关键细节 | Important pieces of information that support the main idea. | 关键细节；重要信息点。 summary | Vocab. 摘要 | A brief statement of the main points of something. | 摘要；概要。 question | Vocab. 问题 | A sentence worded to elicit information or test understanding. | 问题；疑问。 notes | Vocab. 笔记 | Brief records of facts, topics, or thoughts, written down as an aid to memory. | 笔记；记录。 section heading | Vocab. 章节标题 | | A group of words taken from a text or speech and repeated by someone other than the original author. | 引文；引用的话。 example | Vocab. 例子 | A thing characteristic of its kind or illustrating a general rule. | 例子；实例。 explanation | Vocab. 解释 | A statement that makes something clear or provides reasons. | 解释；说明。 claim | Vocab. 论点 | A statement that something is true, often one that other people may disagree with. | 论点；主张。 support | Vocab. 论据支持 | To back up or strengthen an argument or claim with evidence or reasons. | （用证据或理由）支持；支撑（论点）。 supporting detail | Vocab. 支撑细节 | Information that helps to explain or prove the main idea or claim. | 支撑细节；用于解释或证明主要观点的信息。 reason | Vocab. 理由 | A cause, explanation, or justification for an action or event. | 理由；原因。 evidence | Vocab. 证据 | Facts, information, an idea, situation, or problem clear by describing it in more detail. | 解释；阐明。 transition | Vocab. 过渡 | A word, phrase, or sentence that connects one idea or paragraph to the next. | 过渡；用于连接不同观点或段落的词语或句子。 topic sentence | Vocab. 主题句 | A sentence that expresses the main idea of the paragraph in which it occurs. | 主题句；表达段落中心思想的句子。 relevant information | Vocab. 相关信息 | Information that is directly related to the topic or argument. | 相关信息；与主题或论点直接相关的信息。 argument | Vocab. 论证 | A set of reasons given to persuade others that an idea or course of action is right or wrong. | 论证；为说服他人而提出的一系列理由。 paragraph | Vocab. 段落 | A distinct section of a piece of writing, usually dealing with a single theme. | 段落；文章中的一个独立部分，通常围绕一个主题。 draft | Vocab. 草稿 | A preliminary version of a piece of writing. | 草稿；初稿。 revise | Vocab. 修改 | To re-examine and make alterations to (written work). | 修改；修订（文稿）。
